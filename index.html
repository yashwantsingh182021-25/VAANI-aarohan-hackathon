Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use VAANI without internet?
VAANI works with minimal internet. Basic features are available offline, and we use low-bandwidth technology to ensure accessibility even with poor connectivity.
Is my personal data safe with VAANI?
Yes, we follow strict privacy guidelines. We collect minimal data and never share it without your consent. All data is encrypted and stored securely.
Can I use VAANI in my local language?
Currently, VAANI supports Hindi and English. We are working to add more Indian languages soon, including Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, and Gujarati.

Your Privacy & Security
